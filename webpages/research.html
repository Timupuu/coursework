Home Page

Home Page Reference

We loved the simplicity of the AliExpress Home page. The products are displayed in a grid format with high-quality images, making it easy for users to browse through various items. The navigation bar is straightforward, providing quick access to different categories and deals. The colour scheme is also simple. The page itself is very straightforward.


Final Product: Eco Mart's Home Page

Our home page took inspiration from AliExpress' simplicity. We integrated a clean layout of products in a grid pattern. A video was added by us to further improve upon our reference. We also used images and discounted pricings to attract customers. The navigation bar is also based on AliExpress' simple navigation bar.



Product Page

Product Page Reference

The AliExpress product page was further used as the inspiration for our product page. We loved their compact grid layout of products, which also allowed users to see the discount and products at a glance.


Final Product: Eco Mart's Product Page

Our product page was heavily inspired by AliExpress' product page. We used a similar grid layout to showcase our products. Our product page also includes high-quality images, and discounted prices. We tried to improve on it by providing a buy now button on the product containers to make it more user friendly.



Blog Page

Blog Page Reference

For our blog page, we took inspiration from Business Insider's blog page. We loved how simple their blog conainers were. The also properly highlighted each blog with a high quality image. Further more, the font used was also very good. We wanted to integrate a similar layout for our blog page, that consisted of rows of blogs like in their page.


Final Product: Eco Mart's Blog Page

Our blog page was heavily inspired by Business Insider's blog page. We used a similar layout with rows of blog containers, each with a clear image and good font. We tried to improve on it by increasing the font size for better readability and adding more spacing between the containers. We also tried to give the viewers an idea what our blog page was about by using a blue header with a clear title. The image shows a small section of our blog page.



Contact Us Page

Contact Us Page Reference

The Contact Us page was inspired by a blog post from a blog site called JetFormBuilder. Their layout was what caught our eye and the attractiveness of the page. The form was simple yet effective, with clear labels and input fields. The use of a separate space for contact information also made this very user friendly.


Final Product: Eco Mart's Contact Us Page

Since our contact page was already heavily inspired by JetFormBuilder's contact page, we tried to improve upon it by using a cleaner font and adding more spacing between the form fields. We also made the contact information section more simpler by adding a small container for each. Two social media links were also added for better connectivity with our users.



About Us Page

About Us Page Reference

For our About Us page, we took inspiration from a website called Katiz Marketing. We loved their grid layout of team members, and also an image for each. The font that they used was also very good. We thought their layout was very clean and user friendly.


Final Product: Eco Mart's About Us Page

For our about us page, we used a similar grid layout to showcase our team members. Each team member has a high-quality image and a brief description. We tried to improve on it by adding a header section that clearly states our mission and vision. By clicking on a team card, we can get info about them and also a link to their CV.
